Ignored

Integer - for more Information see Damage Calculation

Ignored

-

Filename, including ending of texture variant 1

Filename, including ending of texture variant 2

9

Filename, including ending of texture variant 3

10

...

Any tile must have at least one texture variant, theoretically there is no limit to the number of texture variants a tile can have.
